üí° Understanding Tokenization

Tokenization is the process of splitting text into smaller units called "tokens". These tokens are the basic elements an AI model uses to understand and process language.

üîÑ Different tokenization strategies:

  • Method 1 - Word level: Each word becomes a separate token (9 tokens)
  • Method 2 - BPE: Frequent character pairs form subword tokens (12 tokens)

üí° Practical impact: Models such as GPT rely on BPE to efficiently handle rare or long words by breaking them into recognizable sub-pieces.
